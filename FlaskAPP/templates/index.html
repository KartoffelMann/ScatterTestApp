Motivation

Our application was developed to digitize dementia detection tests that have been in use for years, but did not contain enough quantitative data. The application takes advantage of Apple hardware, coupled with the computational power of Python to facilitate the collection and interpretation of data.



Our approach

A symbol is chosen as the target symbol which the patient will aim to find within the rendered scatter test. Each quadrant of the test is filled with 15 target symbols and a mass of other irrelevant symbols. The patient is then asked to circle as many target symbols as they can while the test administrator times them. The iOS application is able to track the test's total time, the pressure at which a patient circles a symbol, the length of time elapsed in the drawing of a single circle, and the latency between each circle. With this new data, we hope to be able to assist patients who believe they have symptoms of neurodegenerative disease.
