Welcome to the Cancellation Test Dr. {{user.DoctorName}}

The Cancellation Test application was developed to digitize dementia detection tests that at the time of creation were filled with human error and not enough data due to the nature of the tests. The Cancellation Test looks to take advantage of computer processing by allowing to have more forms of data, more precise data, and a higher rate of tests. The test in particular this application is focusing on is as follows: A symbol is chosen as the "correct symbol" and will be the target of the patient, a coordinate plane is drawn and split into four quadrants. Each quadrant is filled with 15 of these "correct symbols" and a mass of other "incorrect symbols". The patient is then asked to circle as many or all "correct symbols" in a defined time. While being able to track time more effectively, we also have the ability to add the pressure at which a patient circles a symbol, the length of time elapsed in the drawing of a single circle, and the latency between each circle. With this new data we hope to be able to get closer and closer to detecting dementia as early as possible.
